# Ajeeb Built-in Functions

## I/O: `print`, `println`, `readFile`, `writeFile`, `writeAppend`, `writeByte`
## String: `len`, `charCode`, `strSet`, `strcmp`, `strcmp_ajeeb`, `itoa`, `chr`, `str_concat`, `substring`, `indexOf`, `contains`, `toUpperCase`, `toLowerCase`, `trim`, `split`, `replace`, `startsWith`, `endsWith`
## CLI & Buffer: `readArg`, `getStateBuf`, `getOutbuf`, `getInt`, `setInt`, `rdPos`, `wrPos`, `allocBuf`
## Char Classification: `isDigit`, `isAlpha`, `isAlphaNum`, `isSpace`
## System: `exec`, `mkdir`
